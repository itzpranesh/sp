What I want in life?

July 22, 2020

(tamil audio)

First be clear on What is needed ? and what I want?

what is needed?

  • good place to live
  • money to pay my bills

What I want?

  • happy life
  • creating a happy world
  • being helpful/useful to the world.

'What I want' and 'what is needed' is completely my choice. I can desire for anything. There is nothing called as good desire or bad desire. I can choose what I want and that is the life I get. I should be ready to face the cause and effect of my choice.

First do what is needed. Then do what I want. But remember, my desire is an endless process.
