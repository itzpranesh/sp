How to live?

July 22, 2020

(tamil audio)

I can not define what life is? It is an infinite possibility. I can make out anything from it. Whatever the situation is, I can choose my way of living. I'm the creator of my life.

When I'm conscious of this, I can create what I want. When I'm not conscious, unknowingly I create nasty things for me and my life feels like accidental.

I can make my life very beautiful or worse. The choice is mine. What I choose is What I get.

  1. Know "what is needed?" and " what I want ?"
  2. Go for it and make it happen.

MY LIFE IS MY MAKING.
