How to make things happen?

July 22, 2020

(tamil audio)

What is possible and what is not possible is not my business.
My business is to strive and work for what matters to me.

Time, situation, luck is not in my control.
I have control over my work and effort. That's it.

I have to do whatever it takes to create what I want.
If my goals requires less work to accomplish it. I have to do less.
If my goals requires more work to accomplish it. I have to do more.

I have to keep my focus and energy unwavering. Cut all the factors that is distracting me from achieving what I want. Create a situation where I can not be distracted even if I want to.

For me it was all types of media ( images, videos, games, movies ). They were constantly deviating my energy and focus. Then I created a system to avoid these things. Now nothing is distracting me. Here and there I consume media but that is not a big deal.

I force myself to work even if I didn't feel to work. Because if that is what it takes to achieve my goals, then I have to do it. If I can't do what it takes, then I can not create what I want.

SHUT UP AND WORK.
EVERYTHING ELSE IS DISTRACTION.
